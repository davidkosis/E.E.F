Five Steps On How To Check NOUN Admission Lists For 2023/2024

Congratulations to all the prospective students who have applied for admission into the National Open University of Nigeria (NOUN) for the 2023/2024 academic session. The next step in your admission journey is to check the NOUN Admission Lists to see if you have been offered provisional admission into your desired program.

NOUN usually releases its admission lists in batches, and you can follow these five simple steps to check your admission status and stay informed about your admission status.

Step 1: Visit the NOUN Admission Portal

The first step is to visit the official NOUN Admission Portal. You can access the portal by typing "www.nou.edu.ng" directly into your web browser's address bar.

Step 2: Navigate to the Admission Section

Once you are on the NOUN website, navigate to the "Admission" section. This section is where the university publishes the list of candidates who have been offered provisional admission.

Step 3: Select the Academic Program

NOUN offers various academic programs, and the admission lists are usually categorized based on these programs. Look for the section on the page that allows you to select your desired academic program (e.g., Undergraduate or Postgraduate).

Step 4: Enter Your Details

On the admission list page, you will be required to enter your Application Number or Matriculation Number in the provided space. Ensure that you enter the correct information to get accurate results.

Step 5: Check Your Admission Status

After entering your details, click on the "Check Admission Status" button. The portal will then display your admission status. If you have been offered admission, congratulations! You can proceed with the necessary steps for acceptance and registration. If your name is not on the current batch, don't worry, as there may be subsequent batches to be released.

Final Thoughts

Checking the NOUN Admission Lists is an important step in the admission process. Be patient and regularly check the NOUN Admission Portal for updates on subsequent batches. We wish all candidates the best of luck in their admission journey and academic pursuits at the National Open University of Nigeria.
