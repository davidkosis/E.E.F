Official News: Uniuyo School of Basic Studies Released Admission List

We are pleased to announce that the University of Uyo (Uniuyo) School of Basic Studies has officially released the Admission List for the 2023/2024 academic session. Congratulations to all the candidates who participated in the Basic Studies programme and have been offered provisional admission into various degree programmes at Uniuyo.

The School of Basic Studies serves as a preparatory programme for candidates who wish to gain admission into the undergraduate degree courses offered by Uniuyo. The rigorous academic training and foundational courses provided by the Basic Studies programme are designed to equip students with the necessary knowledge and skills for success in their chosen fields of study.

How to Check the Admission List

To access the Uniuyo School of Basic Studies Admission List for the 2023/2024 academic session, follow these simple steps:

  1. Visit the official Uniuyo website at www.uniuyo.edu.ng
  2. Navigate to the "Admissions" section on the homepage.
  3. Click on the "School of Basic Studies Admission List" link.
  4. Enter your Basic Studies Application Number in the provided space.
  5. Click on the "Check Admission Status" button.
  6. Your admission status will be displayed on the screen. If you have been offered admission, proceed with the necessary acceptance and registration processes.

We encourage all successful candidates to accept their admission offer and complete the registration process within the stipulated time frame. Failure to do so may result in the forfeiture of the admission offer.

For candidates whose names do not appear on the current admission list, there may be subsequent supplementary lists. We advise you to keep checking the Uniuyo website and your registered email address for updates on supplementary admissions.
